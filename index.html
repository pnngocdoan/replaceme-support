About

ReplaceMe! helps you track household items and get reminded when they need replacing — filters, batteries, toothbrushes, appliances, and more. Scan any item with your camera and we handle the rest.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an item?

Tap the camera button in the center of the tab bar. Point at any item and our AI will identify it and suggest a replacement schedule. You can also search by name.

Will I get reminders?

Yes — once you track an item, ReplaceMe! sends a push notification when it's nearing its replacement date. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

Do I need an account?

Yes. A free account is required to use ReplaceMe!. You can sign up with Google, Apple, or email and password — it only takes a few seconds.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at the address below with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll remove all your data within 7 days.

Is my data private?

Yes. We only store the items you choose to track. We do not sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy below.

Privacy Policy

Data we collect: email address (if you create an account), items you choose to track, and anonymous usage analytics.

Data we do not collect: contacts, location, photos (camera is used locally for scanning only).

Third-party services: Supabase (data storage), Google AdMob (ads), Amazon Associates (product links).

Data deletion: Email us to request full deletion of your account and data.
